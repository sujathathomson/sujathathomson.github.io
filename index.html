Point of View

My Knowledge Management Philosophy

Knowledge management is not just about storing information. It is about helping people act with confidence. Strong KM combines governance, learning, technology, human curiosity, and trust. The best knowledge systems make it easier for people to find what matters, understand why it matters, and apply it in real work.

I believe curiosity is one of the most important qualities in KM. Curiosity helps us ask better questions, understand user needs, identify knowledge gaps, and design systems that serve people rather than simply organize content.
