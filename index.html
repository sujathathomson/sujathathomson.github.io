About

Turning complex knowledge into practical guidance

I’m a Knowledge Management and Learning Specialist with 23 years of experience, including over 14 years at the World Bank Group, working across Controllers, ITS, and Operations. I lead work that helps staff find reliable knowledge faster and use it with confidence.

A Certified Knowledge Manager through the American Productivity & Quality Center (APQC), I bring strong experience across knowledge and content management, learning, and communications — the disciplines that help people adopt new tools and new ways of working. I’m passionate about turning complex knowledge challenges into practical guidance and learning that staff can genuinely use.
